ABOUT ASCENDRIA

Ascendria is a Magical Medieval Kingdom with the Heroes' Square at its heart. From here, you can access the great fiefdoms: Champions of Ascendria, Forges of Ascendria, and Chronicles of Ascendria. Each domain offers its own path to glory, and together, they strengthen the Ascenders in their fight against the evil that terrorizes the realm.

CHAMPIONS OF ASCENDRIA

Step into the Arena where legends are born and glory is eternal. Engage in tactical, turn-based combat where skill, strategy, and timing are your greatest weapons. Rise through the competitive ranks each season, dominate your opponents, and claim the realm's most exclusive rewards. Here, your legacy is not given—it is earned in battle.

FORGES OF ASCENDRIA

Beyond the battlefield, true dominion is forged. In the Forges of Ascendria, claim your own plot of Land and become a master of industry. Cultivate rare resources from your territory and craft the realm's most sought-after goods: legendary weapons, impenetrable armor, and life-altering potions. Here, you don't just supply heroes—you build the very foundation of the Ascendrian economy.

CHRONICLES OF ASCENDRIA

Part of Ascendria's history lies lost in the darkness of its forgotten dungeons. Embark on perilous expeditions to unravel the secrets of a fallen age and gather rare resources, unique magical recipes, and even powerful skill scrolls. The Chronicles are the path for every Ascender to grow stronger, unearth the past, and fund their journey to greatness. Adventure awaits those brave enough to seek it.

THE GOOD FATHER

Said to have always existed, he is the creator of the Ascenders. He drives them to battle and evolve, offering passage to the Arena, the Training Grounds, and soon, the Battlegrounds.

THE BANKER

The Banker oversees all financial matters within the realm, masterfully balancing the markets to ensure the kingdom's long-term economic prosperity.

THE ARTIFICE

The master craftsman responsible for forging nearly everything that can become a powerful ERC-1155 NFT, including Bloodstones, Ascension Stones, and Card Packs.

THE TAVERN KEEPER

The Tavern Keeper sells starter card packs to new adventurers. Within the walls of his tavern, he also serves as a cunning mediator for various games of chance and skill.

THE BLOODLY MERCHANT

A mysterious foreigner who manages the trade of unstable fragments. He acts as a price regulator, overseeing the marketplace where Ascenders negotiate for these volatile resources.

THE ALCHEMIST

The Alchemist brews powerful potions using Tears of the Unlucky. Some of his most potent concoctions can be transformed into valuable NFTs and traded on the main market.

THE HUNTER

The Hunter manages the Expedition Board, guiding most adventurers as they take their first steps towards evolution and glory in the realm of Ascendria.

THE ENCHANTER

The Enchanter possesses the rare ability to imbue items with magic, turning them into unique ERC-7165 NFTs that can be sold on the marketplace.

THE WANDERER

He is responsible for the Unstaking process of all ERC-1155 items, allowing players to bring their digital assets from the game into the real world.

THE RETURNER

The antagonist of the story. The Good Father's lost brother, corrupted in his search for forbidden power. He is responsible for global events that reshape the world itself.
